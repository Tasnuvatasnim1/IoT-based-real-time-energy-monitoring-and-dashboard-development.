Smart Plug Control System

Welcome to your Smart Plug Dashboard. This manual will guide you through all the features and functionality.

1. Dashboard Overview

The dashboard is your main control center for monitoring and managing your smart plug.

Device Status Indicators

  • ON - Device is on and working
  • OFF - Device is off

2. Understanding the Charts

The charts visualize how your energy usage changes over time.

Chart Components

  • X-Axis (Bottom) - Shows the time when measurements were recorded
  • Y-Axis (Left Side) - Shows the measurement values (Watts, Volts, Amps)

3. Power Control

Control your smart plug with these simple steps:

Turning On/Off

  1. Locate the power buttons in the Control Section
  2. Click "Turn ON" to activate the plug
  3. Click "Turn OFF" to deactivate the plug

Safety Features

  • Automatic overload protection
  • Real-time power monitoring
  • Remote access control

4. Energy Monitoring

Understand your energy consumption with these metrics:

Key Measurements

  • Power (W) - Current power consumption in Watts
  • Voltage (V) - Electrical potential difference
  • Current (A) - Flow of electric charge

5. Troubleshooting

Common Issues

  • Device not responding - Check network connection and power supply
  • Incorrect readings - Ensure proper device calibration
  • Connection issues - Restart both the device and router
